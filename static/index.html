Explorer API

Marketplace data is fetched from a public indexer. You can point at your own (the verusexplorer-prod repo runs one). Pricing always comes from your local daemon — see METHODOLOGY.md.

Auto-execute

Auto-fund (lender) and auto-accept (borrower) delegate trade decisions to your browser based on oracle-derived collateral ratios. See TERMS.md §3 for the heightened risk these features carry. The opt-in modal that gated your first activation is recorded in your consent log below.

Oracle sanity check

Cross-check oracle quotes against offer terms before auto-executing. Refuses trades when the implied collateral ratio is outside [0.7×, 1.5×] of the offer's stated ratio, or when the borrower's and lender's quotes diverge by >5%.
